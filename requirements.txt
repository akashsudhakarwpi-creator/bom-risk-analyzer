# ⚡ BOM Risk Analyzer — Smart Speaker

A supply chain risk intelligence dashboard for analyzing semiconductor and component risk across a consumer electronics Bill of Materials (BOM).

Built to simulate the kind of risk visibility work done by supply chain teams at Apple, Google Hardware, Meta, and Sonos.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Consumer electronics manufacturers source 15–50+ components per product from suppliers across Taiwan, China, South Korea, Japan, and the US. Each component carries distinct risk exposure across four dimensions:

- **Single-source exposure** — is there only one supplier capable of fulfilling this component?
- **Geographic concentration** — how exposed is the supply chain to geopolitical or trade disruption by country?
- **Lead time volatility** — how unpredictable are supplier lead times?
- **Substitutability** — how easily can this component be swapped for an alternative?

This tool scores every BOM line item across all four dimensions and surfaces a composite risk score and tier (Critical / Elevated / Low).

---

## 📊 Features

- **Interactive risk heatmap** — all components ranked by composite risk score
- **Geographic exposure donut chart** — annual spend concentration by supplier country
- **Risk radar chart** — multi-dimension breakdown for top 5 highest-risk components
- **Adjustable scoring weights** — shift weight between risk dimensions via sidebar sliders
- **Filterable BOM table** — filter by category, risk tier, or supplier country

---

## 🧠 Risk Scoring Methodology

Each component receives a score (0–100) across four dimensions:

| Dimension | Description | Default Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Single-Source Exposure | Single-sourced = 100; more alternatives = lower score | 35% |
| Geographic Concentration | Taiwan = 90 (TSMC risk); USA = 20 | 25% |
| Lead Time Volatility | High = 90, Medium = 55, Low = 20 | 25% |
| Substitutability | Inverse of sourcing flexibility | 15% |

Composite score = weighted average of all four dimensions. Weights are fully adjustable in the dashboard.

**Risk Tiers:**
- 🔴 Critical: Score ≥ 70
- 🟡 Elevated: Score 45–69
- 🟢 Low: Score < 45

## 💡 Key Findings (Smart Speaker BOM)

- **Audio DSP** (Cirrus Logic) and **Far-Field Mic Array** (Knowles) are the highest-risk components — both single-sourced with high lead time volatility
- **~68% of annual component spend** is concentrated in Taiwan and China — significant tariff and geopolitical exposure
- **Semiconductor and Acoustic categories** carry the highest average risk scores
- **WiFi/BT combo chip** (Qualcomm, 18-week lead time, high volatility) represents a critical connectivity bottleneck
